Your route is not affected by the upcoming TTC cuts.

The frequency you experience right now will stay the same. As this tool is in beta, we aim to add the frequencies of all routes irregardless of it being cut or not in the future. Stay tuned!

Though Line 3 Scarborough is not affected by the upcoming TTC cuts, it is bound to be closed in late 2023.

Line 3 Scarborough has been the backbone for transit in the east end for over 37 years, and despite the lack of rapid transit in the nearby area, it is set to close in less than a few months. Though the TTC is extending Line 2 Bloor-Danforth to Sheppard East, this extension will not be open until 2030. This will force 35,000 daily SRT riders on shuttle buses.

Over the past few years, TTCriders advocated and eventually succeeded in a rapid bus lane to diminish the affects of the closure of the SRT. This rapidway will also include much needed green space for walking and cycling.

However, we still need much more transit in Scarborough. Read our report and sign your name to help!
